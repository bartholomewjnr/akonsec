At Akontombra Senior High School, the curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive and balanced education that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for academic success and lifelong learning. Our curriculum follows the Ghana Education Service guidelines and includes core subjects as well as elective courses across various disciplines. We emphasize critical thinking, practical application, and holistic development through classroom instruction, laboratory work, and extracurricular activities.

  • Core Subjects: English Language, Mathematics, Integrated Science, Social Studies
  • Elective Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Accounting, Business Management, Visual Arts

The academic calendar at Akontombra Senior High School follows the Ghana Education Service standard schedule, designed to provide a balanced and effective learning experience.

  • First Term: Early September to mid-December
  • Christmas Vacation: Mid-December to early January
  • Second Term: Early January to late March
  • Easter Vacation: Late March to mid-April
  • Third Term: Mid-April to late July
  • End of Academic Year Vacation: Late July to early September
  • Examinations: WASSCE and other assessments are typically held during the third term.
