About Akontombra Senior High School

Learn more about our school's history, mission, vision, achievements, and values.

The school was established and absorbed by the Ghana Education Service in January, 1994 through the initiative of the chiefs and people of Akontombra in the then Sefwi Wiawso District who unanimously realized the dynamics of secondary school education on the socio-economic development of individuals and the society at large.

  • To provide a holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social development of our students.
  • To foster a culture of excellence, integrity, and respect within the school community.
  • To prepare students for higher education and responsible citizenship.
  • To promote lifelong learning and personal growth.
  • To be a leading institution in academic excellence and character development.
  • To empower students to become critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible citizens.
  • To create a safe and inclusive learning environment that celebrates diversity.
  • To foster partnerships with parents, alumni, and the community for the betterment of our students.

  • The Girls' Football team was the Champion for Sefwi Zone from 2008 to 2012

  • Established in 1994, the school has grown to become a reputable institution in the region.
  • Over 90% of graduates gain admission to tertiary institutions each year.
  • Strong emphasis on academic excellence, with a focus on STEM subjects.
  • Well-equipped science and computer laboratories to support practical learning.
  • Active participation in extracurricular activities, including sports, music, and drama.
  • Regular workshops and seminars to enhance students' skills and knowledge.
  • Consistent high performance in WASSCE examinations.
  • Numerous awards in national and regional competitions.
  • Active participation in community service and outreach programs.
  • Strong alumni network contributing to the school's growth and development.

Welcome to Akontombra Senior High School. We are dedicated to fostering academic excellence, character development, and a supportive community. Our mission is to empower every student to reach their full potential and become responsible citizens.

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and strong moral principles in all our actions.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in academics and character.
  • Respect: We foster a culture of mutual respect and inclusivity.
  • Responsibility: We encourage accountability and leadership among students.
  • Community: We promote active engagement and service to society.
